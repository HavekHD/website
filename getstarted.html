How-to mine in 5 steps?
1. Download wallet and miner for your platform
2. Launch wallet
3. Go to addresses tab
4. Right click on pre-generated address to copy the address
5. Keep wallet running and launch miner with command
miner --address kryo:5VWDAcZPXZRPJzBncuUCyeCVgJoEoDz7tWeB
